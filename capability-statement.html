Why Projects Stall

Capital can exist. The bottleneck is often structure: weak data, fragmented coordination, unclear risk allocation, and missing execution tools.

Fragmentation

Fragmented planning and weak coordination across multiple institutions.

Data Gaps

Feasibility gaps that surface too late in the development cycle.

Bankability

Structuring gaps that block final approvals and financial close.

Deliverables Library

Institutional outputs designed for decision-makers and lenders.

01. Feasibility & Due Diligence

Pre-feasibility / Screening Note add
Scope includes: constraints scan, data gaps, early risk triggers, and next-step ToR outline.
Options Analysis add
Evaluation of 2–4 solution pathways, including cost/schedule bands, risk comparison, and formal recommendation.
Feasibility Report (Decision-Ready) add
Comprehensive technical and commercial report, implementation plan, annexes, and assumptions log.
Risk Register (Living) add
Active management of probability/impact, mitigation strategies, ownership, and early-warning triggers.
Stakeholder & Institutional Map add
Analysis of decision-makers, approval gates, and institutional coordination protocols.
Bankability Pack add
Investment narrative, model inputs, procurement pathway, and data room starter index.

02. PPP / Delivery Structuring

PPP/Delivery Suitability Note add
Rationale, affordability approach, and risk allocation logic for PPP mandates.
Procurement Strategy & Packaging add
Definition of lots, contract strategy, evaluation logic, and procurement timelines.
Heads of Terms (Term Sheet) add
Key commercial positions for negotiation and approval across stakeholders.
SPV/Operating Model Outline add
Roles, governance structures, interfaces, and performance obligations.
Data Room Index (Minimum Viable) add
Document checklist categorized by workstream and assigned ownership.

03. Master Planning

System / Sector Diagnostic add
Identification of bottlenecks, baseline audit, governance gaps, and investment needs.
Sequenced Delivery Roadmap add
12–36 month roadmap covering work packages, dependencies, and funding gates.
Portfolio Prioritisation Model add
Scoring criteria, sensitivity analysis, and recommended starter set for infrastructure portfolios.
Institutional Alignment Pack add
Mandate definitions, coordination design, and decision cadence protocols.
