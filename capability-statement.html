Capability Statement

InfraDev.Africa | Solutionists

Institutional Infrastructure Advisory

Who we are

InfraDev.Africa is a strategic infrastructure advisory and delivery-support firm focused on making projects feasible, bankable, and executable in African contexts.

Sectors

  • Power systems: transmission and distribution planning, renewables integration
  • Decentralised energy: mini-grids, hybrid systems, storage deployment
  • Urban infrastructure: mobility, housing enabling works, city services
  • Water and sanitation: access systems, program design, delivery monitoring

Core services

  • Master planning and investment roadmaps (cities, corridors, SEZs, utilities)
  • Pre-feasibility and feasibility studies (technical, financial, ESG)
  • PPP and transaction support (structuring, risk allocation, procurement packs)
  • Lender and investor due diligence support (data rooms, technical review)

Why InfraDev

  • Systems thinking: energy, water, health, logistics, and governance as one connected machine.
  • Context fluency: African delivery realities, stakeholder dynamics, and procurement constraints.
  • Boardroom plus field: rigorous documents that survive real sites and messy data.

How we work

Typical engagement formats:

  • Rapid diagnostic (2-4 weeks)
  • Feasibility / structuring (6-16 weeks)
  • Delivery support / PMO (3-18 months)

Primary geography: DRC with pan-African execution through partnerships.

Track Record & Deliverables

Representative experience of leadership and typical outputs

Experience

Selected leadership experience (prior roles):

  • Power networks planning and advisory across Africa (transmission/distribution, renewables integration).
  • Senegal grid stability support (USAID/USEA programme with Mott MacDonald, 2017-2019): technical facilitation, bilingual delivery, data collection and dynamic model support (CYME), and capacity-building recommendations.
  • Luanda integrated planning assignments (master planning / infrastructure coordination).
  • Ghana gas-to-power strategy support (Genser Energy): commercial and delivery-oriented advisory.
  • Company-managed healthcare system transformation (Watu Wetu Health System): multi-phase programme design, governance and performance tracking.

Deliverables

Outputs we produce (examples):

  • Feasibility reports (technical, economic, financial model interface, ESG)
  • Investment notes and decision memos for boards and DFIs
  • PPP / procurement packs: TORs, RFPs, evaluation frameworks, draft risk matrices
  • Implementation plans: phased roadmaps, CAPEX/OPEX logic, governance and reporting systems
  • Dashboards: progress tracking, KPI trees, action logs, stakeholder maps

Delivery principles

  • Evidence-first: assumptions labelled, data gaps logged, decisions traceable.
  • Bankability: we write for lenders, not for theatre.
  • Localisation: designs adapted to procurement, operations, and skills realities.
  • Partner-ready: clear interfaces for OEMs, EPCs, DFIs and public agencies.
